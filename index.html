About Us

Juffali Automotive Company (JACO) has a long partnership relationship with Mercedes-Benz, the world leader in the automotive industry started from the middle of the twentieth century. Throughout this long period, the company maintained the status of the exclusive dealership for Mercedes-Benz cars in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and worked throughout these decades to build and enhance customer loyalty by providing and developing the best services.

There are Mercedes-Benz showrooms in the cities of Jeddah, Riyadh, Dammam, and Abha. Which have been developed to help the customer and visitor get acquainted with Mercedes-Benz cars and their distinctive details.

The company also owns a network of service and spare parts centers throughout the Kingdom, which include a selection of specialized and trained cadres to provide the best possible service for the maintenance of Mercedes-Benz cars with an international level of quality and perfection.
